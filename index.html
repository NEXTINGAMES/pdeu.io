About PDEU

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) is a premier university located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, focusing on energy and technology education. Established in 2007, PDEU has evolved into India's only energy university, dedicated to cutting-edge research and comprehensive education in energy sectors.

Today, PDEU stands as a beacon of excellence with significant national and international recognition, actively contributing to India's AatmaNirbhar Bharat initiative through innovative research and industry collaboration.
