Admission Process

Admissions to SOM are based on national-level entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, GMAT, and MAT. Selection process includes:

  • Written Test Performance
  • Group Discussion
  • Personal Interview
  • Academic Background
  • Work Experience (if any)

Programs Offered

  • MBA (Master of Business Administration)
  • MBA - Energy & Infrastructure
  • MBA - International Business
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • Ph.D. in Management
  • Executive MBA
  • Integrated MBA (BBA + MBA)
